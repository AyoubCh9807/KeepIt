Introduction to KeepIt

Welcome to the KeepIt documentation. KeepIt is your all-in-one personal knowledge management system designed to help you capture, organize, and retrieve information instantly. Whether you are a student, researcher, or professional, KeepIt adapts to your workflow.

Why KeepIt?

In a world of information overload, having a reliable "second brain" is crucial. KeepIt provides a clean, distraction-free environment with powerful features under the hood, ensuring you never lose a great idea again.

Key Concepts

KeepIt is built around three core concepts: Items, Tags, and Boards. Understanding these will help you master the application.
